Privacy

Location?

Its Totally Natural To Feel Strongly About Sharing Your Location With A Website Which You Just Heard About, We Take All The Precautions To Keep Your Location Private, Your Location Is Only Tracked Once When You Open The Website And Its Never Tracked Again In The Whole Session, It Is Done To Show Posts From Your Locality And To Assign A Location To The Posts You Post

Data?

Its The New Age Of Internet, Everyone Is Self Conscious About Their Personal Data, We Don 't Collect Any Personal Data Other Than Your Email, Name And Age, We Do Not Share These Information With Anyone And Respect Your Privacy

Tracking?

We Don 't Track You In Real Life, But Do We Track You Across The Internet? No, We Don 't Track What You Are Doing On Any Other Website Or Anything You Do On This Big Web Of Internet, We Only Track Your Actions Such As Posts And Interests To Serve You With Relevant Posts And Keep You Up To Date With Everything
